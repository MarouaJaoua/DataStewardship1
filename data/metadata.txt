Description of Files:

* characteristics_siegl: The characteristics of each country including the iso identifiers
* gni_per_capita: The index gni per capita
* income_groups: The income groups including the low, lower, upper and high groups
* life_expectancy_male_female: The life expectancy for both genders (male and female).
* mergedData : The data obtained after merging all sources
* preprocessedData : The data obtained after preprocessing the merged data
* who_data : The data from world health organization (WHO)
